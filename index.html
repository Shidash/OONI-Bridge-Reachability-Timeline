Tor Bridge Reachability Timeline

Explanation

  • Reports are ordered chronologically and grouped by country and pluggable transport
  • Row at top summarizes results for pluggable transports of a given type in a given country
  • Click the + sign to see bridge-specific reports
  • Hover over reports to see data below and other reports from same day highlighted

Color Codes

  • Green = Bridge is reachable (or in a summary row, all bridges are reachable)
  • Red = Bridge is blocked (or in a summary row, all bridges are blocked
  • Yellow = Some of the bridges in the set are reachable and some are blocked
  • Orange = Bridge is down (in both control and test cases)
  • Purple = Inconsistent or invalid results (up in test cases and down in control, incomplete, etc.)
  • Grey = No data
